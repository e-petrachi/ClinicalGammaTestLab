More INFORMATION

Blood Count:

Tests that provide information about the components of blood including red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets. A blood test that measures your blood sugar levels.

Tests for Anticoagulants

These blood tests help to determine your risk for coronary artery disease and guide your treatment.

Lipid Blood Tests:

Blood tests that provide information about the amount of cholesterol levels in your blood.

Tests Risk of Coronary Disease:

Electrolyte levels are useful in detecting kidney, heart and liver disease, and the effects of certain medications.

Blood Sugar Tests:

Blood tests that provide information about the amount of cholesterol levels in your blood.

Thyroid Blood Tests:

A series of blood tests that provides information about the function of your thyroid gland. Tests that provide information about the waste products in the blood excreted by the kidneys.
